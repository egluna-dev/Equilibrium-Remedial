    About Us

    Aden Petersen

    I started practicing massage when I was little on my family and have always had a strong connection to natural medicine and massage. I completed my 'Diploma of Remedial massage' in 2012 and have been practicing ever since. Having experience in both clinical and day spa environments, I offer the best of both worlds in my unique style.

    Our Commitment

    My remedial massage incorporates advanced techniques with stretching and mobilisation to achieve outstanding results for my customers. I am very passionate about my work and love what I do, which my customers can tell. No matter the massage you choose, I will look after you and make sure you have a wonderful experience.
