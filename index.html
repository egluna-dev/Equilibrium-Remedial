    What We Do

    We at Equilibrium Remedial aim to help you find your inner and outer harmony using our experience and intuition to find the right treatments and massage for you. We incorporate modern and traditional massage techniques in a unique way to provide a memorable experience and help you find pain and stress relief or just to bliss out for a while... Best of all, we can come to you! Our convenient mobile massage services come to your home or office as well as treatments at our home clinic (by appointment only). With everything brought to you in the comfort of your own home you can enjoy a rewarding and effortless experience.

    Our Mission

    We are in the business of helping people feel good and have aligned ourselves with the goal to make the best quality therapies in the industry available to more people. We understand that it can be difficult to find time to unwind and take care of ourselves, that is why Equilibrium Remedial is focused on the convenience of 'mobile massage therapies' as well as offering after hours appointments to better suit your needs...

    ...Our Mission

    All of the therapists at Equilibrium Remedial are trained and passionate about what we do. We want to show that massage is more than just a luxury but a tool to have a better life; empowering our clients to be the best they can be without the burden of aches, pain and stress weighing them down. We believe in kindness and helping others. A portion of our profit is donated to those in need. People can donate massage treatments to others through our ‘Donate a massage’ page Our trained therapists mission is to provide you a wonderful experience, completely tailored to you.

    COVID-19 Safety

    At Equilibrium Remedial we are entirely dedicated to ensuring not only the relaxation and healing of our clients, but also their health and safety. These protocols are what is keeping our practices open and running safely.
